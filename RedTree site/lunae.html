Lux Lunae: Lupus Ad Me

Kheit, a wolf caught in a jaw trap, falls for the woman who nurses him back to health. His prayers are answered when he's granted the opportunity to be human for a short time in order to win her heart, but he's in turmoil. A deceitful new wolf has laid claim to Alpha. Can Kheit save his pack and win her in the time allotted? Or will he miss the opportunity to be with the one he loves?
